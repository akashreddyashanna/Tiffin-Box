The story of tiffin box

In the 1990s, Tiffin Box was more than just a restaurant; it was a place where tradition came alive. Inspired by the founder's grandmother, who packed delicious meals in her tiffin box, we set out to recreate that magic.

Tiffin Box was where flavors from home met the heart of Indian cooking. Each dish told a story, connecting us to our roots and bringing back memories of homemade goodness. Guests didn't just come for food; they came for a taste of tradition and the warmth of nostalgia.

At Tiffin Box, every visit was a journey back in time, where familiar flavors sparked conversations and created new memories. It wasn't just a restaurant; it was a place where stories were shared and traditions honored.

Welcome to Tiffin Box, where the spirit of the 90s lives on in every bite, and every meal is a celebration of the connections that make us feel at home.

Our Food

Tiffin Box offers a culinary journey through the heart of India, with dishes that honor tradition and celebrate flavor. From aromatic curries to savory street snacks, each bite is a testament to our commitment to quality and taste.
